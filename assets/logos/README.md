# Brand Logo Assets

## Instructions

Place your brand logo files (PNG, SVG, or JPG) in this directory with the following naming convention:

### Required Logo Files:
1. `starbucks.png` - Starbucks logo (green circle with white siren)
2. `nike.png` - Nike swoosh (black)
3. `amc.png` - AMC Theatres logo (red circle)
4. `target.png` - Target bullseye (red)
5. `sephora.png` - Sephora flame (black)
6. `sweetgreen.png` - Sweetgreen logo (green)
7. `delta.png` - Delta triangle (red)
8. `classpass.png` - ClassPass chain links (blue)
9. `chipotle.png` - Chipotle logo (red circle with pepper)

### Format Requirements:
- **Format**: SVG or PNG with transparent background (preferred) — JPG also supported
- **Size**: At least 200x200px for square logos, proportional for others
- **Quality**: High resolution, crisp edges
- **Aspect Ratio**: Preserve original proportions

### Once Uploaded:
The logos will automatically be integrated into the phone mockup displays with:
- Preserved aspect ratios
- No distortion or stretching
- Consistent padding
- Visual balance
- Brand-accurate colors
