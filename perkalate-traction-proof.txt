Slide 1: Traction
Headline:
Perkalate is moving from product build to network formation.

Body:
Perkalate has crossed the line from a rewards concept into a commercial network that brands can join, test, and use for verified customer rewards.

Proof bullets:
- 100+ brands expected to be onboarding by end of June 2026, creating the first supply side of the Perkalate rewards network.
- Working consumer app with account syncing, reward discovery, and a permissioned verification surface.
- Brand-side campaign infrastructure for creating rewards based on verified customer behavior.
- POS / Shopify redemption work underway, turning eligibility from a marketing signal into a checkout action.

Speaker note:
Do not overstate this as signed revenue unless the contracts are signed. The point is commercial pull, onboarding velocity, and a credible path to network liquidity.

Slide 2: Commercial Momentum
Headline:
Early demand is coming from brands that need verified behavior, not another points system.

Body:
Perkalate's wedge is not generic loyalty. It is verified eligibility: brands define the behavior that matters, Perkalate verifies it, and rewards can be claimed and redeemed where commerce happens.

Proof bullets:
- Small-business onboarding motion: build density quickly with 100+ brands and repeatable reward templates.
- Sports and venue conversations: major sports teams are exploring Perkalate as a way to connect fan behavior, sponsor value, and commerce redemption.
- Commerce-first infrastructure: the same system supports DTC, Shopify, local merchants, CPG partnerships, sports, venues, and sponsor-funded rewards.
- Clear buyer: brands pay to reach verified customers and prove the reward or partner outcome.

Speaker note:
The sports-team pipeline is powerful because it shows larger enterprise relevance, but the deck should still frame the near-term wedge as repeatable brand onboarding and reward activation.

Slide 3: Why It Matters
Headline:
The hard part is not launching a reward. It is proving who should get it.

Body:
Most rewards tools help brands publish offers. Perkalate gives brands the missing infrastructure layer: permissioned customer data, eligibility verification, redemption, and attribution.

Proof bullets:
- For brands: launch rewards against verified purchases, loyalty status, account behavior, location signals, and partner eligibility.
- For customers: claim rewards they were already eligible for without hunting across scattered apps, cards, receipts, and accounts.
- For partners: prove what happened instead of relying on manual, delayed, or vague attribution.

Speaker note:
This is the bridge from traction to market size. If 100+ brands can onboard quickly, Perkalate has a credible path to become a rewards-eligibility layer, not just a campaign tool.
