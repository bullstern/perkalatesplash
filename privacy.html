Further, if you remove the App’s access to access to loyalty programs or services, we will be limited in our ability to provide you with offers or other opportunities relating to those loyalty programs or services.

We also may use these technologies to collect information about your activities over time and across third-party websites, apps, or other online services (behavioral tracking). The technologies we use for automatic information collection may include:

  • Cookies (or mobile cookies). A cookie is a small file placed on your smartphone. It may be possible to refuse to accept mobile cookies by activating the appropriate setting on your smartphone. However, if you select this setting you may be unable to access certain parts of our App.
  • Web Beacons. The App and our emails may contain small electronic files known as web beacons (also referred to as clear gifs, pixel tags, and single-pixel gifs) that permit Perkalate, for example, to count users who have visited those pages \[or opened an email\] and for other related app statistics (for example, recording the popularity of certain app content and verifying system and server integrity).\]
  • Session Replay. The App contains technology that allows us to review your interactions with the App. Session replay will capture mouse movements, click targets, scroll positions, and page navigation, Session replays capture mouse movements, click targets, scroll positions, and page navigation. If we store a session using session replay, we will remove all personal information, text inputs, passwords, and sensitive form fields. We also do not log keystrokes.
